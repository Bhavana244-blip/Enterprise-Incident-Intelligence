Incident ID: INC-001
Service: Payments API
Severity: High

Issue:
Transactions were failing and latency exceeded 5 seconds during peak traffic hours.

Root Cause:
Database connection pool exhaustion caused by a sudden increase in concurrent requests.

Resolution:
Increased database connection pool size, enabled query caching, and scaled read replicas.

Preventive Actions:
Improved traffic spike alerts and implemented auto-scaling thresholds.
